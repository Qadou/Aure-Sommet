Purchasing Centre

The purchasing center, developed by the African Union of Real Estate Promoters, represents a major advancement in the real estate field in Africa. This innovative tool aims to enhance transparency and efficiency in transactions between sector actors. By providing increased visibility to all industrialists, developers, and other stakeholders, it fosters price competitiveness and broadens available choices. Additionally, it acts as a financial guarantor, securing transactions and facilitating exchanges between African industrialists and developers. This initiative addresses a crucial need, particularly in overcoming trust challenges that can arise, particularly between African industries and developers, and due to high banking transaction costs in Africa.
